About Connection

Connection is a new social media platform, where you can find what's happening in your neighbourhood and connect with others based on the activities(BUZZ) happening.
Connection can be used from finding the 11th player of Cricket team for your cricket match with friends at local park, to getting notified of the little Rap battle happening in your college campus.

It is a Instant, Localised and Private platform for interaction.

Aims to decrease the need for the Blue screen(Mobile Phone) and increase the real world social connections enjoyed in real world environment.

In one sentence if we have to describe connection, it would be

"twitter for you neighbourhood! where you don't just share your emotions but make new connections alongside strengthening the old ones you have with the help of day to day activities on Connection application platform."

How to use Connection?

Connection is an Android application, that can be used with just 2 steps.

Step 1: Create a post on connection (c/a BUZZ) of what's happening with you.
Step 2: Wait for the others to see your post and they will respond to it by sending you a connection request if interested & if you want to connect with them, just accept the connection request send by them.
